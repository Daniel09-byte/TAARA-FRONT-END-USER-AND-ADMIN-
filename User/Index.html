Our Mission

TAARA is a community-driven initiative dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in Tabaco City and nearby areas. We believe in compassionate rescue, responsible adoption, and education for humane treatment.

About Us

Founded in 2021, TAARA began as a small volunteer group responding to calls about animals in distress. Over time, our network of rescuers, fosters, and adopters grew into a reliable support system for animals in need.

We coordinate emergency rescues, provide basic veterinary care, and match animals with loving homes. Our team also leads outreach efforts to promote spay/neuter, vaccination, and responsible pet care.
